Our Commitment to Safety

Your well-being is our priority. All support groups are:

  • Anonymous: Share without revealing personal identifying information.
  • Moderated: Facilitators ensure discussions remain respectful and supportive.
  • Secure: We use technology to protect your privacy and data.
  • Judgment-Free: A space where all feelings are acknowledged and validated.

We are continuously working to expand our group offerings. If you have suggestions or are interested in becoming a volunteer moderator, please contact us.
